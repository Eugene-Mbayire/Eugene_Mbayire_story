Welcome to My Life Story

A journey of strength, love, and hope – by Mbayire Eugene

Quick Story Resume



My name is Mbayire Eugene. I'm 32 years old, born in DRC. My mother passed away when I was four. I didn't grow up in a wealthy family, but I was raised with love and courage. I'm currently studying software engineering, while working as a driver since 2017. I married my lovely wife Kayitesi Yvonne in October 2023, and we had our first son, Imena Nganji Mbayire, in March 2024.

On My son's first Birthday

When I held Imena Nganji Mbayire in my arms for the first time on the 09th March 2024, I knew my life had changed forever.


This photo means everything to me — a moment of pure blessing: my loving wife, and our precious baby boy, Imena Nganji Mbayire. The day you came into our lives, I felt something inside me shift. I held you close, and I knew right then that you were going to carry not just my love, but my name, my dreams, and my hopes for a better future. I named you MBAYIRE — my own name, a name I proudly received from your grandfather. It carries history, strength, and responsibility. It means a lot, my son, and I want you to know it is not just a name — it’s a legacy. Never take it for granted. Carry it with pride, and always make sure you live in a way that honors it. I hope one day, your children will inherit it too, just as I did. Your mother gave you the name Imena — a powerful and beautiful name. Always live in a way that reflects its meaning. Be strong in character, and grounded in purpose. My son, I wish you a life full of peace, wisdom, humility, and boldness. Grow into a man who is kind and fearless, respectful and brave. We will raise you with all the love we have, and I promise to always be there — not just to guide you, but to learn from you too. You are already a blessing, and I know your life will continue to be one to many. This is only the beginning, my boy — but what a powerful beginning it is. We love you endlessly.
